Our Story

Hydration with a Higher Purpose

From Colombia to the world, a story of love, science and commitment to wellness.

How our inspiration began

INAAM began with a deeply human intention: a husband seeking relief for his wife's struggle with fibromyalgia, and a team's shared desire to reimagine what healthy hydration and the future of the beverage industry could look like.

From that personal mission grew a vision for a cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable way to restore the body — not just quench its thirst.

Roots in Biodiversity

Rooted in Latin America's lush biodiversity and refined through eight years of research and development in Colombia, now with global headquarters in Boca Raton, FL.

INAAM represents a new standard for functional hydration — a kind of modern alchemy where science and nature converge.

Its philosophy is simple: wellness, sustainability, and innovation should coexist. From ethically sourced botanicals that protect local ecosystems to 100% recyclable packaging that minimizes waste, every element reflects that belief.

What does INAAM mean?

Reward, divine gift, generosity, blessings... Hindi culture describes this word as the supreme power we have to achieve what we want.

How to Use Your Elixir

Four simple steps to transform water into a functional botanical experience.

1

Choose your flavor

Select from our Ginger, Yellow Fruit, Red Berry, Watermelon-Mint, or Lychee-Hibiscus elixirs.

2

Measure the elixir

Pour ½ ounce (15ml) of concentrate into a glass.

3

Add water

Top with 8-10 oz of sparkling or still cold water.

4

Mix and enjoy

Stir gently, add ice if desired, and enjoy your functional botanical beverage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you prepare INAAM Elixir?
Mix ½ ounce (15ml) of elixir with sparkling or still water. You can adjust the intensity to your taste. You can also use it in mocktails or recipes.
How many servings does one Elixir bottle yield?
A 500ml bottle yields approximately 33 servings, making it much more economical and sustainable than a single-use beverage.
Does the Elixir have the same benefits as Botanical Soda?
Yes, the Elixir contains the same concentrated botanical extracts. When diluted, you get a beverage with the same functional benefits: 0% sugar, adaptogens, and prebiotics.
Can I use the Elixir for cooking?
Absolutely! INAAM Elixir is perfect for marinades, dressings, desserts, and mocktails. Its intense botanical flavor enhances any recipe.

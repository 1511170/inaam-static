Why Glass Matters

Glass possesses unique circular economy properties:

  • Infinite recyclability — unlike aluminum (which degrades with each cycle) and plastic (which downcycles), glass can be recycled indefinitely without structural or chemical degradation
  • Inert composition — does not leach chemicals into beverage content, even with acidic botanical formulations
  • Reuse potential — amber glass bottles are suitable for home repurposing (storage, DIY projects) before recycling

Preparation Versatility: Beyond the Glass

INAAM Elixir’s concentrated format enables preparation creativity impossible with ready-to-drink beverages:

Standard Preparation

  1. Fill a 250ml glass with cold water (still or sparkling)
  2. Add 15ml of INAAM Elixir (approximately one tablespoon)
  3. Stir gently and add ice to preference
  4. Garnish with fresh fruit, herbs, or edible flowers
